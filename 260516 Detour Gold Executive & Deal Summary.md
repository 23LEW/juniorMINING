## DEAL STRUCTURE & VALUATION ANALYSIS

### Transaction Mechanics

**Deal Type:** All-Stock Merger/Arrangement
- **Instrument:** Plan of Arrangement (Canadian corporate law structure)
- **Voting:** Kirkland Lake shareholders & Detour Gold shareholders separately approved
- **Regulatory:** Completed under Toronto Stock Exchange (TSX) and Canadian securities law

### Valuation Framework

**Exchange Ratio Basis:**
- **Ratio:** 0.4343 Kirkland Lake Gold shares per Detour Gold share
- **VWAP Reference:** 20-day volume-weighted average price through November 22, 2019
- **Premium Calculation:** 29% premium over weighted average of both companies' stock

**Consideration Value:**
- **Total Deal Value:** CAD 4,890 million (CAD 4.9 billion)
- **USD Equivalent:** USD 3,680 million (USD 3.68 billion)
- **Exchange Rate Implicit:** ~1.33 CAD/USD
- **Shares Issued:** 77,217,129 Kirkland Lake Gold shares
- **Implied Price per Detour Share:** CAD 4.90B / (77.2M shares ÷ 0.4343) ≈ CAD 18.06/share equivalent

### Premium Analysis & Deal Rationale

**Acquisition Premium:**
- **29% VWAP Premium** calculated over 20-day period before November 22, 2019
- Suggests fair/reasonable M&A premium for junior-mid tier gold M&A (~25-35% typical range)

**Kirkland Lake Strategic Rationale:**
1. **Reserve Base Addition:** +15.41 million ounces Au (major reserve accretion)
2. **Reserve Life Extension:** +8 years to Kirkland's reserve portfolio
3. **Production Scale:** +600,000 oz/year annual production (1.5M+ oz pro forma)
4. **Cost Synergies:** USD 75-100 million pre-tax annual savings
5. **Geographic Diversification:** Addition of large Ontario asset to portfolio
6. **Long-Life Asset:** 22+ year mine life provides operational continuity

### Shareholder Economics (Post-Transaction)

- **Interpretation:** Pre-deal Kirkland was ~73% of combined entity value; Detour ~27%

## DEAL RATIONALE & VALUE CREATION THESIS

### Why Kirkland Lake Wanted Detour (Buyer Perspective)

1. **Scale:** +600,000 oz/year production = major step toward 1.5M+ oz/year target
2. **Reserve Quality:** 15.4 M oz proven/probable + 4.4 M measured/indicated = 19.8 M oz base
3. **Geographic Fit:** Ontario asset fits Kirkland's growing Canadian/North American portfolio
4. **Long Life:** 22-year reserve life = stable long-term cash generation
5. **Cost Synergies:** USD 75-100M annual savings (operating/capex rationalization)
6. **Valuation:** All-stock deal conserves cash; issues ~77M shares (~15% dilution)

### Why Detour Gold Shareholders Sold (Seller Perspective)

1. **Shareholder Pressure:** Recent proxy contest = shareholder dissatisfaction with independent growth prospects
2. **Operational Challenges:** 
   - Open pit ore grades declining (approaching economic limit)
   - Cost structure uncompetitive vs. Kirkland's operations
   - Management uncertainty (new CEO/CFO May-June 2019)
3. **Market Premium:** 29% VWAP premium = fair value for troubled junior producer
4. **Certainty of Value:** Stock deal → immediate liquidity + exposure to larger producer
5. **Lack of Alternatives:** No other visible buyers; Kirkland offered best alternative to standalone path

### Value Creation Mechanisms

**Kirkland Lake Projected Synergies:**
- **Operational Synergies:** Different cost structures → potential USD 30-40M annually
- **Capital Synergies:** Consolidated mine planning, infrastructure sharing → USD 20-30M annually
- **Overhead Reduction:** Head office consolidation, duplicate function elimination → USD 20-30M annually
- **Total Target:** USD 75-100M pre-tax annually (14-16% of Detour's estimated EBITDA)

**Valuation Impact (Rough Estimate):**
- If USD 75-100M annual synergies valued at 6x multiple
- Synergy value: USD 450-600M (9-16% of USD 3.7B deal value)
- Suggests deal designed with expectation of meaningful synergy capture
