## Corporate History Summary

**Phase 1 (1982-2006):** Junior exploration in Quebec  
**Phase 2 (2006-2014):** Development and production (Canadian Malartic); IPO/TSX listing  
**Phase 3 (2014):** Acquired by Agnico Eagle + Yamana Gold; Osisko Gold Royalties spun out as separate company  
**Phase 4 (2015):** Merger of 5 companies → "new" Osisko Mining Inc. (developer focus)  
**Phase 5 (2020):** Osisko Gold Royalties spins out mining assets → Osisko Development created  
**Phase 6 (2024):** Gold Fields acquires Osisko Mining for CAD 2.16B; company delists
