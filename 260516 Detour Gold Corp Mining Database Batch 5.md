## 3. DETOUR LAKE PROJECT TIMELINE

### Discovery & Development History
**1974** – Gold deposit discovered by Amoco during base metal exploration program

**1979** – Rights holder agreements: Amoco Canada partnered with Dome Mines and Campbell Red Lake Mines Ltd. (later merged to form Placer Dome)

**1983-1999** – Original mine operation under Placer Dome
- Estimated reserve at opening: 3.5 million ounces Au
- Operated as open pit initially, then underground

**2006** – Detour Gold Corporation formed to redevelop dormant Placer Dome property
- Property being maintained as exploration project by Pelangio Mines Inc. prior to acquisition

### Pre-Production Technical Milestones

**June 30, 2010** – Feasibility Study Technical Report filed
- Project economics: $1.2B capex
- Reserve estimate: 14.9 million ounces Au
- Mine life: 21 years
- Breakeven: US$625/oz gold price
- IRR: 11.3% (post-tax)
- NPV (5%): USD 469 million @ USD 775 gold price
- Expected production: 300-500k oz/year

**October 2010** – Updated resource estimate
- Proven & Probable Reserve: increased to 4.8 million oz (baseline for development)

**January 7, 2011** – Draft Comprehensive Study Report (environmental assessment)

**November 2011** – Final Comprehensive Study Report (Canadian environmental authority - IAAC)

**2011-2013** – Construction phase
- Kiewit Corporation contracted for infrastructure
- 135 km, 230 kV transmission line construction
- Major capital investment and project development

### Production Milestones

**January 12, 2013** – Initial low-grade ore processed in mill

**February 18, 2013** – First Gold Pour
- Ceremonial gold bars poured (~2,000 oz)
- Demonstrates successful commissioning

**August 7-12, 2013** – Commercial Production achieved

**December 31, 2013** – Full first year production in operation

### Resource Base Evolution

**2016** – Mineral reserve estimate: 16.9 million ounces Au
- Substantial exploration program growth from 4.8 Moz (2007)

**December 31, 2018** – Year-end resource estimate
- **Proven & Probable Reserves:** 15.4 million ounces Au
- **Measured & Indicated Resources:** 4.4 million ounces Au
  - Includes 534,000 oz addition from Zone 58N
- **Inferred Resources:** 1.3 million ounces Au
  - Includes 136,000 oz addition from Zone 58N
- **Reserve Life Index:** ~22 years (based on LOM plan throughput)
- **Resource assumptions:** $1,200/oz Au (main), $1,300/oz (Zone 58N)

**Production Performance (2018)**
- Annual production: 621,128 ounces Au
- Q4 2018 production: 158,200 ounces (best quarterly result)
- All-in sustaining cost (AISC): USD 1,158/oz sold
- Revenue: USD 776.0 million (610,672 oz @ USD 1,268/oz avg realized price)

**Production Performance (2019)**
- Annual production: 601,566 ounces Au
- AISC guidance: USD 1,100-1,175/oz

## 4. KIRKLAND LAKE GOLD M&A DEAL DETAILS

### Transaction Summary
- **Announcement Date:** November 25, 2019
- **Definitive Agreement Signed:** November 24, 2019
- **Completion Date:** January 31, 2020
- **Time to Close:** ~68 days

### Deal Structure & Valuation
- **Deal Value:** CAD 4.9 billion (CAD 4,890 million)
- **Consideration:** All-stock arrangement
- **Exchange Ratio:** 0.4343 Kirkland Lake Gold shares per Detour Gold share
- **Shares Issued:** Approximately 77,217,129 Kirkland Lake Gold common shares
- **Valuation Date:** 20-day VWAP calculation through November 22, 2019

### Premium Analysis
- **VWAP Premium:** Approximately 29% over 20-day volume-weighted average price (DGC + KL, to November 22, 2019)

### Strategic Rationale & Economics
- **Detour Lake Characteristics:**
  - Large-scale, long-life Canadian asset
  - 2019 production: ~600,000 ounces Au annually
  - High-quality ore base with significant expansion potential

- **Combined Entity Benefits:**
  - Added 15.41 million ounces Au to Kirkland's reserve base
  - Extended reserve life index by 8 years
  - Pre-tax annual cost savings: USD 75-100 million
  - Production increases to 1.5+ million oz/year (pro forma)

### Shareholder Ownership Post-Close
- **Kirkland Lake Gold shareholders:** 73% (pro forma)
- **Detour Gold shareholders:** 27% (pro forma)

### Regulatory & Approval Timeline
- **Shareholder Approval:** Kirkland Lake shareholders approved deal
- **De-listing:** Detour Gold shares delisted from TSX on or about February 3, 2020
- **Post-Acquisition Status:** Detour became wholly-owned subsidiary of Kirkland Lake Gold
